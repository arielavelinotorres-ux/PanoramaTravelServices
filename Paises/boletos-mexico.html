Destination overview

Mexico City surprises with museums and vibrant neighborhoods, while the Riviera Maya offers turquoise waters and Mayan ruins. A complete destination for rest and exploration.

Must-sees

  • Historic center and Chapultepec
  • Riviera Maya and cenotes
  • Guanajuato and San Miguel de Allende

Quick tips

  • Bring sunscreen and light clothing for the coast
  • Book tours early in high season
  • Combine city and beach for a balanced trip
