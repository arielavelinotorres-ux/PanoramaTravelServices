Destination overview

Tokyo and Kyoto blend modern and traditional. Bullet trains make it easy to move between multiple cities in one trip.

Must-sees

  • Tokyo, Shibuya, and Asakusa
  • Kyoto and its temples
  • Mount Fuji and Hakone

Quick tips

  • Buy a JR Pass if you visit multiple cities
  • Book hotels near stations
  • Try local markets and street food
