Destination overview

Madrid, Barcelona, and Seville combine history, architecture, and nightlife. Spain is best enjoyed on foot, with great food and lively plazas.

Must-sees

  • Gran Via and museums in Madrid
  • Sagrada Familia and neighborhoods in Barcelona
  • Seville and the Alhambra in Granada

Quick tips

  • Buy tickets early for monuments
  • Use high-speed trains between cities
  • Book hotels in central locations
